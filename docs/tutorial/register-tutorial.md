## Registration Form

To create your account, please fill in the following fields:

### Email Address

Enter your email address. This will be used for account verification and notifications.

[you@example.com](mailto:you@example.com)

### Username

Choose a unique username for your account. This will be your identity on the platform.

### Password

Create a strong password to secure your account.

**Requirements:**
- At least 8 characters  
- Includes uppercase and lowercase letters  
- Includes at least one number  
- Consider using special characters (e.g., `!`, `@`, `#`, `$`, `%`, `^`, `&`, `*`)

### Confirm Password

Re-enter your password to confirm. This ensures that you have entered it correctly.

### Phone Number (Optional)

Enter your phone number if you wish to receive SMS notifications or two-factor authentication.

## Tips for a Strong Password

- Avoid common words or sequences like "password123".  
- Mix letters, numbers, and symbols.  
- Consider using a password manager to generate and store strong passwords.  
- Regularly update your password to enhance security.
